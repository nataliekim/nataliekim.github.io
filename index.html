Coffee Project
HCI research @ Cornell

I worked with another student to design an interface/tool to help make coffee production cost inputing easier for Latin American coffee cooperatives. Our primary goal was to improve the lack of transparency within the industry regarding production costs to help ensure that prices remain profitable for farmers.

Spotify Case Study
personal project

While taking Intro to Digital Product Design, a class offered by Cornell AppDev, I decided to write a case study on Spotify. In my study, I identify a core problem with music discovery and design a solution by exploring the problem space, interviewing users, and creating different prototypes.

SpectrumScores
content strategy freelance

I was contracted to create content for an upcoming website that matches members of the LGBTQ+ community with medically competent & empathetic professionals. My goal was to create language that gave the right cues for each possible action a user wants to take, as well as create an information system that emphasizes human & simple language.
